Complete Guide
MHT-CET Exam Info
Everything about the exam — schedule, syllabus, CAP process, counselling & more
📅

Exam Schedule 2025

  • Exam Date: May 2025
  • Result: June 2025
  • CAP Round 1: July 2025
  • CAP Round 2: August 2025
  • CAP Round 3: August 2025
  • CAP Round 4: September 2025
📝

Exam Pattern

  • PCM paper: 200 marks
  • 150 questions (MCQ)
  • No negative marking
  • 3 hours duration
  • Physics: 50Q, Chemistry: 50Q, Maths: 50Q
  • Based on Class 11 (20%) & 12 (80%) syllabus
🎓

Eligibility

  • 12th pass with PCM (50% for OPEN, 45% reserved)
  • Maharashtra domicile or HSC from Maharashtra
  • Age: no upper limit
  • AICTE approved courses only
💰

Fees Range

  • Government colleges: ₹60K–₹1.5L/year
  • Govt-Aided: ₹1L–₹1.8L/year
  • Private-Aided: ₹1.5L–₹3L/year
  • Private/Deemed: ₹3L–₹5L/year
  • EWS/reserved: fee concessions apply
⚙️

CAP Process

  • Register on cetcell.mahacet.org
  • Upload documents (12th marksheet, category cert.)
  • Fill option form (rank preferences)
  • Seat allotment published after each round
  • Report to allotted college within deadline
📋

Documents Needed

  • 12th marksheet & passing certificate
  • MHT-CET scorecard
  • Domicile certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for EWS)
  • Aadhaar card

Laboratories & Technical Infrastructure
🖥️
Computer Labs
High-performance workstations (i7/Ryzen), latest OS, licensed software (MATLAB, AutoCAD, Oracle). COEP & VJTI have GPU-enabled AI labs with 100+ nodes.
🤖
AI / ML Labs
Dedicated AI labs at PICT, VIT Pune, SIT, DJSCE. NVIDIA GPUs (RTX 3080/A100), deep learning frameworks, Raspberry Pi kits, IoT boards.
⚡
Electronics & VLSI Labs
Oscilloscopes, signal generators, FPGA boards (Xilinx/Altera), microcontrollers (Arduino, STM32). COEP & WCE Sangli have VLSI design labs.
⚙️
Mechanical Labs
CNC machines, 3D printers (FDM + SLA), hydraulics, thermodynamics rigs, fluid mechanics, material testing machines. COEP & WCE Sangli are best-equipped.
🔬
Research Centres
COEP: National Centre for NDE. VJTI: Centre for Excellence in Telecom. PICT: Centre for AI Research. SIT: Symbiosis Centre for Applied AI.
🚀
Incubation / Startup
