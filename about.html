About the Personality Development Club

The PDC aims to nurture students with character, competence, and culture. We organize workshops, self-awareness sessions, public speaking exercises, and mentorship programs to promote confidence, communication, and leadership.

Vision

To nurture students with strong character, competence, and cultural values, empowering them to lead meaningful lives rooted in wisdom and integrity.

Mission

To support self-awareness, protect against self-destructive habits, and foster a community where students respect and support each other.
